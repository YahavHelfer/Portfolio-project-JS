Expense calculator

This JavaScript project is designed to help users manage their finances by tracking expenses. The Expense Calculator allows users to input their expenses, categorize them, and view a summary of their spending. With a clean and responsive interface, users can easily add, edit, and delete expenses while keeping an eye on their budget. This tool is perfect for anyone looking to gain better control over their financial habits.
