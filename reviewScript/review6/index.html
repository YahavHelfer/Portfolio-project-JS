Fruits Scramble Game

This engaging JavaScript-based Fruits Scramble Game challenges players to unscramble mixed-up fruit names. With a colorful and responsive design, the game offers a fun and educational experience for all ages. Players can guess the correct fruit name from scrambled letters and receive instant feedback on their guesses. The game encourages quick thinking and improves vocabulary, making it an entertaining and beneficial activity.
